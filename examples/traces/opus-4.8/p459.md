- Subsets of `{1..n}` with weight ≤ k are nodes.
- Allowed moves: flip one bit (add/remove a dancer) or swap adjacent differing bits (shift `++i`/`--i`).
- We need a Hamiltonian path starting at empty, ending at a singleton, then remove it.

Recursive (reflected-Gray-like) construction where `G(n,k)` goes from empty to `{n}`:
- `G(n,k) = G(n-1,k) , +n , reverse(G(n-1,k-1))` for `k≥2,n≥2`.
