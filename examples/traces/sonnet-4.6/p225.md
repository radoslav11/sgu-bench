- A knight attacks squares that are 2 rows away and 1 column away (or vice versa, but within same row distance of 2)
- Actually, knights attack: (±1, ±2) and (±2, ±1)

- Knights in row i can only attack knights in rows i+1 (by moving ±1 row, ±2 cols) and row i+2 (by moving ±2 rows, ±1 col)
- So we need to track the last 2 rows of placements
