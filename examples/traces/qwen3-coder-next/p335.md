The key observation is that in most grid configurations, the cop can catch the thief in exactly `d` moves, where `d` is the Manhattan distance between their initial positions.    - **General Case (Non-2x2 Grids)**: The cop can always catch the thief in `d` moves if the cop moves first, or in `d` or `d+1` moves if the thief moves first (specifically, `d` moves if `d` is even, otherwise `d+1` moves).
