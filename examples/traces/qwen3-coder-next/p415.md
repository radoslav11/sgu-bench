We are given n, x, and a list of n distinct coin denominations.
 We know Vasya has exactly one coin of each denomination.
 We need to find which coins are *absolutely necessary*. That is, a coin is necessary if every subset of coins that sums to x must include that coin.

 However, note the problem statement: "which of his coins are absolutely necessary to do so (i.e., he is forced to abandon them)".

 Actually, reading the example:
